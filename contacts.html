About us

Founded in 2018 by wedding enthusiast and event expert Anna Williams, M&W was born out of a passion for creating unforgettable moments of love and celebration. What started as a small, one-woman operation quickly grew into a trusted wedding agency known for its personalized approach, attention to detail, and creative vision. With over 6 years of experience in the wedding industry, our team has successfully planned and coordinated more than 200 weddings — from grand luxury ceremonies to intimate elopements. We believe every love story is unique, and our mission is to bring each couple’s dream day to life with elegance, joy, and ease. Our agency is based in London, but we proudly serve couples across the country.
